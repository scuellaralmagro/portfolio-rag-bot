# About Sergio

Sergio Cuéllar Almagro is a software developer based in Marbella (Málaga), Spain, working
across AI automation and full-stack web development. He specializes in turning manual
processes into scalable, agentic AI architectures — designing multi-agent systems and
retrieval pipelines that move from prototype to production — while also building robust web
applications from frontend to backend. His technical foundation spans Python, TypeScript,
Node.js, React/Next.js, and PHP, alongside database, automation, and machine-learning
tooling, which lets him deliver projects end to end.

## What Sergio does

Sergio builds cohesive agentic systems and automates complex workflows with N8N and
LangChain/LangGraph. He combines hands-on Python and TypeScript experience with a deep
understanding of RAG and RAFT retrieval architectures, and he integrates LLM features into
modern web platforms — pairing smooth user experiences with powerful backend workflows. On
the web side he works with React, Next.js, Node.js, and PHP (Laravel/Symfony), and on the
data side with PostgreSQL/Supabase, pgvector, and MySQL.

## Sergio's background

Sergio has a strong technical foundation in Big Data and machine learning, built through
intensive training and hands-on client work as a freelance web developer and data analyst
before moving into agentic AI and automation roles. He is comfortable owning a project from
data and backend through to a deployed frontend, and he brings a data-analysis mindset
(sentiment analysis, KPIs, web analytics) to product decisions.
